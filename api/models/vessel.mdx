## Overview

The Vessel model represents boats or ships used to transport packages between islands in the Maldives. Each vessel has a name, destination island, registration number, and operational status. Vessels are assigned to packages for shipment tracking.

## Database Table

**Table name:** `vessels`

## Vessel Status

The `status` field is a boolean that indicates whether the vessel is operational:

- `true` (1): Vessel is active and available for assignments
- `false` (0): Vessel is inactive or unavailable

## Notes

- The `regno` (registration number) field is nullable, allowing vessels to be registered before obtaining official registration
- Vessels are typically associated with specific islands, indicating their primary route or destination
- The relationship between vessels and packages is established through the `vessel_id` foreign key in the packages table
- User accounts can be linked to specific vessels through the `boatid` field, allowing vessel operators to manage their shipments
- Vessel schedules track departure times and help customers monitor when their packages will depart

## Related Models

- **Package**: Packages are assigned to vessels for transportation
- **Schedule**: Vessels have schedules that define departure and arrival times
- **User**: Users (vessel operators) can be associated with specific vessels
