# Customer Guide

This guide helps customers navigate the NaaleMV system to track their packages, view vessel schedules, review transactions, and manage their account settings.

## Getting Started

### Registration and Login

Customers are typically created by vessel operators during the shipment creation process. Your account is associated with a unique **Customer Number** (`CustNumber`).

**Login Route:** `GET /login`

After authentication, you'll be redirected to your customer dashboard.

<Info>
  Customer accounts require email verification before accessing most features. Ensure you verify your email address after registration.
</Info>

### First Time Setup

Upon first login:
1. Verify your email address (check your inbox for verification link)
2. Review your customer profile information
3. Update your password in settings if needed

## Customer Dashboard

The dashboard provides an overview of your shipment activity and latest package status.

**Route:** `GET /customer/dashboard`

**Controller:** `CustomerController@dashboard` (source: `app/Http/Controllers/CustomerController.php:51`)

**View:** `resources/views/customer/dashboard.blade.php`

### Dashboard Features

#### Shipment Statistics

The left panel displays:
- **Total Shipments** - All packages you've created
- **Shipped** - Packages currently in transit or loaded (not collected)
- **Total Collected** - Packages successfully delivered

**Source:** `CustomerController@dashboard` (source: `app/Http/Controllers/CustomerController.php:55-60`)

#### Package History

The right panel shows your most recent package with a visual timeline:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Created Shipment">
    When the package was first created by the vessel operator.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Loaded to Vessel">
    Package loaded onto the vessel (e.g., "Loaded to Dhoni Boat").
  </Step>

  <Step title="Boat Departure">
    Vessel departed from origin to destination. This step shows:
    - Departure date from vessel schedule
    - Route information (from → to)
    - Active status if departure date has passed
  </Step>

  <Step title="Docked">
    Vessel arrived at destination island.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Package Collected">
    Package delivered and collected by recipient.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**View:** `resources/views/customer/dashboard.blade.php:46-113`

<Note>
  If you have no shipments, the dashboard will display "No order found".
</Note>

## Viewing Package Status

Track all your packages and their current status from the transactions page.

**Route:** `GET /customer/transaction`

**Controller:** `CustomerController@transaction` (source: `app/Http/Controllers/CustomerController.php:78`)

**View:** `resources/views/customer/transaction.blade.php`

### Package Information

Each transaction displays:
- **Package ID** - Unique identifier for the package
- **Status** - Current status (LOADING, LOADED, COLLECTED)
- **From/To** - Origin and destination islands
- **Customer Address** - Delivery address
- **Created Date** - When the package was created
- **Vessel** - The vessel handling your shipment

Packages are listed in descending order (most recent first).

**Source:** `CustomerController@transaction` (source: `app/Http/Controllers/CustomerController.php:80`)

### Understanding Package Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|----------|
| **LOADING** | Package is being prepared, items are being added |
| **LOADED** | Package is finalized and loaded on the vessel |
| **COLLECTED** | Package has been delivered and collected |

## Checking Vessel Schedules

Stay informed about vessel departure times and routes.

**Route:** `GET /customer/schedule`

**Controller:** `CustomerController@schedule` (source: `app/Http/Controllers/CustomerController.php:73`)

**View:** `resources/views/customer/schedule.blade.php`

### Schedule Information

Each schedule entry shows:
- **Vessel Name** - Name of the boat
- **Vessel Contact** - Contact number for the vessel operator
- **Departure Date** - When the vessel departs
- **Dock Island** - Final destination island
- **Visiting To** - Islands the vessel will visit en route
- **Status** - Current status (active or COMPLETE)

Schedules are displayed in descending order (most recent first).

**Source:** `CustomerController@schedule` (source: `app/Http/Controllers/CustomerController.php:75`)

<Tip>
  Active schedules (status ≠ COMPLETE) indicate vessels currently operating. Contact the vessel operator if you have questions about your shipment.
</Tip>

## Reviewing Transactions and Payments

View detailed transaction history including payment status and amounts.

**Route:** `GET /customer/settlement`

**Controller:** `CustomerController@settlement` (source: `app/Http/Controllers/CustomerController.php:83`)

**View:** `resources/views/customer/settlement.blade.php`

### Settlement Details

For each package, you can view:
- **Package ID**
- **Payment Status**
  - **PAID** - Payment completed (cash or transfer received)
  - **CREDIT** - Payment pending (Pay On Delivery)
- **Total Amount** - Sum of all items (quantity × unit price)
- **Package Details** - Origin, destination, and address

### How Payment Status is Calculated

The system checks the `receivables` table to determine payment status:
- If a receivable record exists for your package → **PAID**
- If no receivable record exists → **CREDIT** (payment on delivery)

**Source:** `CustomerController@settlement` (source: `app/Http/Controllers/CustomerController.php:88-103`)

<Info>
  The total amount is calculated by summing all shipment items:
  ```
  Total = Σ(item.quantity × item.unit_price)
  ```
</Info>

## Managing Account Settings

**Controller:** `CustomerController@setting` (source: `app/Http/Controllers/CustomerController.php:106`)

**View:** `resources/views/customer/setting.blade.php`

### Changing Your Password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
    Go to `/customer/setting` from the navigation menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill Password Form">
    Enter:
    - **Old Password** (`old_pass`) - Your current password
    - **New Password** (`new_pass`) - Your new password

    Click "UPDATE" to save your new password.

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  If the old password doesn't match or the new passwords don't match, you'll see an error: "Password doesent match". Verify your entries and try again.
</Warning>

**Success Message:** "Password Change Successfull"

**Source:** `SettingController@ChangePassword` (source: `app/Http/Controllers/SettingController.php:96-108`)

## Common Customer Scenarios

### Tracking a New Shipment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Receive Notification">
    When a vessel operator creates a package for you, your account is either created or updated with the new package.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Dashboard">
    Log in and visit your dashboard to see the latest package status.
    
    **Route:** `GET /customer/dashboard`
  </Step>

  <Step title="Monitor Progress">
    Watch the package timeline to see when:
    - Package is loaded
    - Vessel departs
    - Vessel arrives at destination
    - Package is ready for collection
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Verifying Package Contents

While customers cannot view individual item details through the web interface, the settlement page shows the total amount. To verify contents:

1. Visit **Transactions** (`/customer/settlement`)
2. Check the total amount for your package
3. Contact the vessel operator if you have questions about items

### Checking Payment Due

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Settlement Page">
    Go to `/customer/settlement` to view all transactions.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Payment Status">
    - **PAID** = No payment due, already settled
    - **CREDIT** = Payment due on delivery
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Total Amount">
    Note the total amount for any CREDIT transactions - this is what you'll pay upon collection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Understanding Departure Status

On your dashboard, the package timeline shows whether the vessel has departed:

- **Active (highlighted)** - Departure date has passed, vessel is en route
- **Inactive (grayed out)** - Departure date is in the future

**Source:** `CustomerController@dashboard` (source: `app/Http/Controllers/CustomerController.php:65-66`)

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot Access Dashboard

**Problem:** Redirected to login when accessing customer routes.

**Solution:** 
1. Ensure you're logged in
2. Verify your email address - most customer routes require verification
3. Check that your account rank is set to "customer"

**Middleware:** Routes under `auth` and `verified` middleware groups (source: `routes/web.php:29`)

### No Packages Showing

**Problem:** Dashboard shows "No order found".

**Solution:** 
- You may not have any packages yet
- Contact a vessel operator to create a shipment for you
- Packages are associated with your `custid` - verify this matches your customer record

**Source:** `CustomerController@dashboard` (source: `app/Http/Controllers/CustomerController.php:54`)

### Password Change Fails

**Problem:** "Password doesent match" error.

**Solution:**
1. **Old password incorrect** - Verify you're entering your current password correctly
2. **New passwords don't match** - Ensure `new_pass` and `con_pass` are identical

**Validation:** `SettingController@ChangePassword` (source: `app/Http/Controllers/SettingController.php:96`)

### Total Amount Seems Wrong

**Problem:** Settlement total doesn't match expectations.

**Solution:** 
- The total is calculated from all items in the package
- Each item contributes: `quantity × unit_price`
- Contact the vessel operator to review item details
- Items can be edited before the package is marked as COLLECTED

**Calculation:** `CustomerController@settlement` (source: `app/Http/Controllers/CustomerController.php:98-101`)

### Cannot See Vessel Contact

**Problem:** Need to contact vessel operator but don't have their number.

**Solution:**
1. Visit **Schedule** page (`/customer/schedule`)
2. Find the schedule for your vessel
3. Note the **Vessel Contact** field

**Source:** Schedule includes `vessel_Contact` from vessel operator (source: `app/Http/Controllers/SettingController.php:48`)

### Package Lifecycle

1. **LOADING** - Vessel operator creating package, adding items
2. **LOADED** - Package complete, on vessel
3. Vessel departs (based on schedule)
4. Vessel arrives at destination
5. **COLLECTED** - Package delivered to customer

### Payment Workflow

#### Pay On Delivery (POD)
- Payment status: **CREDIT**
- Pay when collecting package
- No receivable record created during loading
- Payment recorded when collected

#### Pay Now
- Payment status: **PAID**
- Payment made when package created
- Receivable record created immediately
- Can be CASH or TRANSFER (with slip)
